About Me

My name is Will, and I'm passionate about raising awareness for the negative effects of social media on teen mental health. I created this website to share information, resources, and ways to get involved in the fight against social media's harmful impact. If you want to get in contact, send me an email at will@socialmediaissues.me!

How can you get involved?

There are many ways you can help raise awareness and make a difference. You can sign my petition, share this website with others, donate to mental health organizations, and most importantly, take care of your own mental health and encourage those around you to do the same!
